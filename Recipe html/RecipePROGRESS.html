Grandmas Potato Dumpling Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Cold Mashed Potatoes
  • 1 Cup of Flour
  • 1 Egg
  • 1 Tablespoon Sour Cream

Directions

  1. Combine mashed potatoes into flour (easiest way is to use clean hands to mix).
  2. Beat together egg and sour cream in seperate bowl.
  3. Fold egg and sour cream into flour mixture.
  4. Cover with a dish towel and let rest for 10 minutes.
  5. Roll out the rested dough and cut into coin sized bites.
  6. Cook in boiling salted water for 10 minutes or until they float to the top.
  7. Fry with melted butter and salt.

    8. Tips:
    • For parties larger then 4 quadruple the batch, they go very fast!
    • Frying with garlic butter makes them even more delicious!
    • Can be easily made gluten free with gluten free flour!
